Customs Brokerage Services

Seamless customs clearance for fast & compliant cargo movement.

Our licensed customs experts manage documentation, HS classification, duty assessment and compliance to ensure your shipments clear quickly and without penalties.

Your cargo, cleared with confidence.

Customs compliance is critical to smooth international trade. Our dedicated documentation and customs team works closely with importers, exporters, customs officials and partner agencies to ensure every shipment is processed correctly and on time.

We eliminate delays by pre-checking documents, validating HS codes, preparing declarations and coordinating directly with port/airport authorities.

  • Import & export customs clearance (Air & Ocean)
  • HS classification, valuation & duty advisory
  • Filing of shipping bills, bills of entry, EDI & documentation
  • Certificates, licenses & regulatory compliance support
  • Liaison with customs, DGFT, FSSAI & allied agencies

Accurate documentation

From invoice and packing list checks to compliance with DGFT, FSSAI and plant quarantine norms, we ensure paperwork is complete and compliant.

Precise HS & duty classification

Our experts interpret tariff schedules, trade agreements and exemption notifications to determine the correct HS code and duty structure.

Coordination with agencies

We liaise with customs, port/airport authorities, DGFT, FSSAI, plant quarantine and other regulators to speed up clearance.

How it works

Our Customs Clearance Workflow

1. Document verification

Commercial documents are reviewed for accuracy, compliance requirements and regulatory mandates based on cargo type.

2. HS classification & duty calculation

We determine the correct HS code and evaluate duty structure, exemptions and additional compliance needed.

3. Filing & customs coordination

Shipping bill/Bill of Entry filing, EDI processing, clarifications and coordination with customs officials are managed end-to-end.

4. Release, duty payment & delivery

After assessment & release, we support duty payment, out-of-charge and arrange cargo movement to warehouse or delivery point.
