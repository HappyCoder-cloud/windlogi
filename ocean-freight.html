Port, CFS & ICD Expertise

How it works

Our Ocean Freight Workflow

1. Cargo Planning & Routing

Based on cargo size, commodity and urgency, we propose optimized FCL/LCL routes with carrier options and transit schedules.

2. Booking & Documentation

We manage BL/MBL, SI/EDI filing, VGM, certificates, and customs compliance at origin and destination.

3. Container Movement

Coordination across ports, CFS, ICDs, carriers and transporters ensures seamless loading, sailing and unloading cycles.

4. Delivery & Status Updates

Real-time tracking, milestone updates and POD sharing after successful cargo delivery.
