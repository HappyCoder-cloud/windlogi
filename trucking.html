Trucking & Inland Transport

Pan-India trucking solutions for reliable first-mile and last-mile delivery.

Windwalker Logistics connects ports, airports, ICDs, CFS and customer facilities with safe, on-time road transport tailored to your cargo profile.

Road transport that keeps your supply chain moving.

Our trucking and inland transport services are designed to bridge the gap between factories, warehouses, ports and end customers. From full truck loads to part loads, we plan routes and vehicle types to match your cargo, timelines and cost expectations.

With a strong network of fleet partners and dedicated control on key corridors, we provide reliable connectivity and visibility across major industrial and consumption hubs.

  • FTL & LTL road transport across major Indian routes
  • First-mile pickup from factories, ICDs, CFS and ports
  • Last-mile delivery to distributor, retailer or end customer
  • Containerized, trailer and open-body vehicle options
  • GPS-enabled vehicles and milestone-based updates

Right vehicle for every load

From containerized trucks and trailers to smaller vehicles for city distribution, we select the right equipment to balance safety and cost.

First-mile & last-mile expertise

Coordinated pickup from ports, ICDs and warehouses plus last-mile delivery to factories, depots, DCs and customers, keeping your supply chain aligned.

Route planning & safety focus

We plan routes with transit time, road conditions and security in mind, supported by drivers briefings, secure halts and tracking.

How it works

Our Trucking & Inland Transport Flow

1. Requirement & lane study

We review your shipment volumes, lanes, delivery windows and loading constraints to define the right transport model.

2. Vehicle planning & scheduling

Vehicles are scheduled in line with your production and dispatch plans, with buffer time for port and warehouse procedures.

3. Execution, tracking & coordination

Our operations team coordinates loading, transit and unloading, sharing updates on departure, en route progress and arrival.

4. POD & performance review

Proof of delivery and other documents are shared promptly, and we review KPIs like on-time performance and damage-free delivery.
