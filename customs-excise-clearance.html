Customs & Excise Clearance

End-to-end customs & excise support for compliant trade operations.

From licenses and bonds to audit support and regulatory advisory, Windwalker Logistics helps you stay aligned with evolving customs and excise rules while keeping your cargo moving.

Compliance that protects your cargo and your business.

Customs and indirect tax regulations are constantly changing. Our customs & excise specialists work as an extension of your team to interpret notifications, manage licenses and ensure every shipment is aligned with current laws and procedures.

We support importers, exporters and manufacturers with ongoing advisory, documentation review and representation before authorities, reducing the risk of delays, penalties or disputes.

  • Assistance with registrations, licenses & bonds
  • Excise & indirect tax documentation review
  • Support for audits, queries & assessments
  • Regulatory updates and impact analysis
  • Advisory on duty benefits, exemptions & schemes

Licenses, bonds & registrations

Guidance and assistance for obtaining and maintaining relevant customs and excise registrations, licenses, bonds and approvals needed for smooth operations.

Audit & documentation support

Structured record-keeping, documentation checks and preparation for departmental audits or inspections, helping you respond confidently.

Regulatory & duty advisory

Ongoing updates on changes in customs, GST and excise regulations with practical guidance on how they impact your import, export or manufacturing flows.

How we assist

Our Customs & Excise Support Approach

1. Understanding your operations

We review your import/export flows, manufacturing activities, HS codes and existing registrations to map your compliance landscape.

2. Compliance gap & risk analysis

Potential gaps or process risks are highlighted and prioritized, with recommendations for corrective actions and process improvements.

3. Implementation & documentation

We help implement SOPs, documentation controls and record-keeping practices to align your operations with regulatory expectations.

4. Ongoing advisory & representation

Continuous support for audits, notices, clarifications and changes in law, acting as your partner before customs and excise authorities.
