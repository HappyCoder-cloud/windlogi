Air Freight Services

Fast, reliable and secure global air freight solutions.

When speed and reliability matter the most, our air freight team ensures your cargo moves on time, with complete visibility and compliance across all major trade lanes.

Your trusted partner for time-critical shipments.

Windwalker Logistics provides airport-to-airport, door-to-door and multimodal air freight services designed around your timelines and cargo requirements.

From handling export/import documentation to coordinating with global airline partners, our experienced operations team ensures speed, compliance and reliability on every shipment.

  • Express, priority, deferred and consolidated air freight options
  • Handling of general, high-value, pharmaceutical & sensitive cargo
  • Customs clearance at origin and destination
  • Multi-leg routing & trans-shipment planning
  • 24x7 tracking and proactive status updates

How it works

Our Air Freight Process

1. Cargo Assessment

We evaluate shipment size, urgency, handling needs and airline availability to propose the optimal routing.

2. Booking & Documentation

Our team handles bookings, MAWB/HAWB creation, export documents and compliance checks.

3. Airport Handling & Uplift

Screening, palletizing, airline coordination and cargo acceptance are managed end-to-end.

4. Tracking & Delivery

Real-time updates and final POD shared upon cargo arrival or doorstep delivery.
